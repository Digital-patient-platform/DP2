Features of EPON

Self Service Terminal

EPON as a Self service terminal Self Check-In is possible for all appointments, including OPD or regular doctor visits,lab tests and vaccinations. EPON Self Service Terminals offer a practical way to streamline hospital check-in, reduce waiting times, provide better patient experience.

Progressive Web Application

A cutting-edge solution from EPON advances web applications. This application has a responsive, modern UI implementation. The primary goal of creating a progressive web application is to enable cross-platform compatibility across a variety of devices.

Health Record

A step towards making healthcare digitised EPON provide a solution to maintain your lab and vaccination record in your mobile application.

Push Notification

In this fast world, we often forget our appointments, so we will be coming up with a solution, Users will be notified for their lab and vaccination appointment or once the lab report is ready for pick up.
